Our marvelous Easter holiday

Since I've been here in Mahanoro, I've never spent my holiday here. But this year due to coronavirus we couldn't go home so we had to stay here for the sake of our health. Staggeringly, it turned up well. I mean, it didn't turn to the way that I was expected. I was assuming that it will be the toughest time that I have ever had in my life, but it was the opposite of that. Having a jovial friends and stuff are so wonderful. We had a lot of fun during that week because we did some activities together.

On Easter monday



Bonfire

We had some activities: in the morning we just stayed at home. But in the evening we went on the beach and we did VITSILY. Vitsily is a Malagasy game and It's quite fun. On Monday night, we had a bonfire in our yard. This was the most unforgettable moment because I have never done that before. We had some drinks, hot milk, and everyone was dancing. That night we slept at 08:30.

On Tuesday 14 of April

On Tuesday afternoon, we played werewolf and it was so good. Everyone seemed to participate with the discussion. Such was an excellent time.

On Wendesday 15 of April

This was a frustrating day because It was raining the whole day. So we couldn't do anything.

On Thersday 16 of April

This time one of our teachers taught us how to use a camera on our phone. We learn something like how can we get the high quality of photo using grid and angle etc.

On Friday 17 of April


Long walk

On Friday morning, we went for a long walk through the beach, unfortunately the weather was so oppressive, so we couldn't go farther. And also it's tiring because we haven't done that for a long time. When we approach the half of our trip we take a little break.


Having some rest
