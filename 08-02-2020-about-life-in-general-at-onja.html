How is life at Onja look like?

In general life at onja is really good because everyone is cool and the accommodation is really nice we are feel comfortable living here.

Activity

we are 20 students at Onja and we shared into 5 groups. we have our own chore per group everyday and we have to make sure that our chore is completed at the end of the day.

Collaboration

Working together is the most important things at onja because we work hard and we still have our chores that have to be done everyday so we need to collaborate to complite them.
We are keen on helping each other.

Those are the chores which we have to do at onja:

  • Washing up
  • Sweeping the classroom, kitchen and yard
  • Washing pots
  • Sifting rice
  • Fetching water
